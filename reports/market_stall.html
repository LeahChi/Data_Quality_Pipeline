Data quality investigation of a market stalls dataset

Introduction

This report analyses the data quality of a market stalls dataset.The data is (c) Leeds City Council, 2025, https://datamillnorth.org/dataset/leeds-markets-e519w. This information is licensed under the terms of the Open Government Licence.It applies Roy Ruddle’s six-step workflow to systematically identify structural issues, missing data, inconsistencies, and characteristics of the dataset.
